## 🌍 Overview
EcoTrack is a web-based application that helps users track their carbon footprint while promoting healthier lifestyles through gamification.

It transforms daily activities into measurable impact, encouraging users to build better habits through streaks, badges, and visual progress tracking.

---

## 🎯 Problem
Many people struggle to maintain healthy lifestyles and are often unaware of how their daily actions impact both their health and the environment.

Existing solutions are often boring, complex, or not motivating enough, leading to low engagement.

---

## 💡 Solution
EcoTrack makes sustainability and health tracking **engaging and rewarding** by turning it into a game-like experience.

Users can:
- Track daily activities
- Earn health points
- Maintain streaks
- Unlock achievement badges
- Visualize progress through charts

---

## ✨ Features
- 📊 Carbon footprint tracking
- 💪 Health score system
- 🔥 Daily streak tracking
- 🏆 Achievement badges
- 📅 Monthly analytics dashboard
- 🎨 Clean modern UI

---

## 🧠 Theme Alignment
This project aligns with **UN SDG 3: Health and Well-being** by encouraging users to adopt healthier lifestyles while being mindful of their environmental impact.
